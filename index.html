All the things from all levels

  • Horizon 5: Purpose and principles
  • Horizon 4: Vision
  • Horizon 3: Goals
  • Horizon 2: Area of focus and accountabilities
  • Horizon 1: Current projects
  • Ground: Current Actions

Process

Either actionable or non actionable

Non-action

  • Trash it
  • Someday / maybe (tickler file(review))
  • References (store it)

Actionable

  • Do it* (if it'd take < 2 minutes)
  • Delegate it
  • Defer it

Organize

Lists of

  • Next Actions
  • Waiting For
  • Calendar
  • Projects
  • Someday/Maybe
  • References
  • Contextual Lists (ie Errands)

Review

  • Weekly Review
  • Gather and process (what worked, what didn't)
  • Follow up on Waiting For list items
  • Get your system Clean, Clear, Current, and Complete

Do

  • Gotta Do things to get them done
  • How to choose what to do?
    • Context
    • Time
    • Energy
    • Priority

Can anybody think of a Methodology where...

  • Multiple lists of things to do in various stages (to do, errands, by projects, waiting for, someday...)
  • A review period
  • Different priorities

And the development cycle is quicker?

Hint...

  • lists of various develop stages (in progress, backlog, qa, done)
  • Reoccuring review times on semi-weekly periods (where you set projects to be accomplished) and reassess priorities
